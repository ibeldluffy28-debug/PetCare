Help Center

Find answers to frequently asked questions

How do I add a new pet?

You can add a new pet by clicking the "Add Pet" button in your profile under "My Pets".

How do I update my profile?

Go to your profile page and click "Edit Profile" to update your personal information.

How do I manage my bookings?

You can manage your bookings under the "Booking" section from the main navigation.

How do I contact support?

You can contact our support team through the "Help Center" link or the chat option on the dashboard.
